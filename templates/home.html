What is YHMI?

  • YHMI is a web tool to identify enriched/depleted histone modifications and enriched histone/chromatin regulators for a gene list.

  • YHMI uses ChIP-chip datasets of 15 histone modifications and 83 histone/chromatin regulators.

    6 histone acetylation H3K4ac, H3K9ac, H3K14ac, H3K14ac (in H2O2), H4ac, H4ac (in H2O2)
    7 histone methylation H3R2me2a, H3K4me1, H3K4me2, H3K4me3, H3K36me3, H3K79me2, H3K79me3
    1 histone ubiquitination H2BK123ub
    1 histone variant H2AZ
    83 histone/chromatin regulators (in 25C and 37C) Ada2, Brn1, Esa1, Gcn5, Rsc4, Rxt2, Snf1, Swc1, Yaf9, ... See more.

  • The identification results are shown both in figures and tables.
